Gap 1 root cause: YouTube mobile uses yt-button-shape, ytm-button-renderer,
  yt-chip-cloud-chip-renderer, ytm-chip-cloud-chip-renderer, div[role=button],
  yt-formatted-string for interactive affordances — not <a> tags. These elements
  do not inherit the `a` color rule and may hardcode their own colors.
  The active pivot bar selector uses [aria-selected="true"] which may be stale —
  YouTube may have shifted to [aria-selected] without a value, or a different
  attribute. Also target .iron-selected and [selected] for robustness.

Gap 2 root cause: YouTube's unauthenticated page (m.youtube.com when not signed in)
  renders ytm-browse with a different child structure. The sign-in prompt container
  and unauthenticated shell elements are not covered by existing selectors.
  Likely elements: ytm-browse[page-subtype="home"] in unauthenticated state,
  .sign-in-container, ytm-account-controller, .ytm-auth-screens,
  yt-upsell-dialog-renderer, and generic card/panel containers.
